Introduction

What is this Blog for? The reasons may change. Infact, I hope they change as I also grow. However, here is the best answer I have for now.

I believe it is an outlet. What for? Well, to begin with, writing freely in a more direct and personal tone as if I am addressing the reader directly is unexpectedly liberating. Perhaps I can even develop my communication skills. Secondly, my curiosity seems to inevitably lead me to exhilarating topics that I feel the need to share and discuss. Blogging may be a solution.

Finally, it'd be kinda cool if the posts will were read and helpful to someone out there :)

Topics to expect

  • Coding/Computer Science: Any particularly interesting or annoying topics I come across on my journey as a developer.
  • Productivity: Always looking to improve efficiency/productivity so anything that I find and works for me I'll post.
  • Philosophy/Psychology: Definitely more of a hobby interest of mine but has a surprising amount of useful practical knowledge.
  • Random: Anything interesting is fair game really. This could be to do with mechanical keyboards to badminton to a great podcast I found. Who knows.
So lets get started.
