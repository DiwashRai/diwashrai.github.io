Blog

What is this Blog for? The reasons may change. Infact, I hope they change as I also grow. However, here is the best answer I have for now.

I believe it is an outlet. What for? Well, to begin with, writing freely in a more direct and personal tone as if I am addressing the reader directly is unexpectedly liberating. Perhaps I can even develop my communication skills. Secondly, my (annoyingly) overly active and introspective mind inevitably leads me to exhilarating topics that I feel the need to share and discuss. Blogging may be a solution.

Finally, I must admit, I also have a hope that the posts will eventually be read and actually appreciated by someone out there.
