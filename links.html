A page full of links/resources that I find useful, interesting or just enjoy. Easy way for me to show people what I'm into.

Youtube Channels/Series

  • Algorithms series by HackerRank. Short video series that go over the some fundamental coding algorithms.
  • Data structures series by HackerRank.Series that go over fundamentals of coding data structures.
  • TechLead. Videos from an ex-Google tech lead mainly about programming. Sometimes just about life too. Interesting to watch during downtime.

Podcasts (or similar audio content)

I absolutely adore podcasts. Enough that I'll dedicate a section solely for them. Really helps me to make use of time even more. I can continue learning and being entertained whilst doing chores, running, travelling etc.

  • Hardcore History by Dan Carlin. All of the episodes are great but in particular 'The Wrath of the Khans' is a must listen.
  • Waking up with Sam Harris - Episode #147: Stephen Fry. Stephen Fry is someone I respect and this episode shows more of his intelligent, humble but down-to-earth way of thinking.
  • Joe Rogan Podcast. The variety and calibre of guests that Joe is able to get on his show seems unmatchable. Not only that, but he has this ability to get them to open up; all of them from academics to athletes to comedians alike. Guests include the likes of Elon Musk, Neil deGrasse Tyson and Jordan Peterson. Personal favourites of mine were David Goggins and Elon Musk.
  • Freakonomics and Planet Money. Both are interesting finance/economics related podcasts. Nothing too technical, but very interesting.
